Kids App Privacy Policy


Effective from: 22/10/2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kids App(Epanova), a platform designed to help parents discover and book exciting events for their children. At Kids App, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

To provide the best experience for you and your family, Kids App collects the following types of information:

  • a. Account Information: When you create an account on Kids App, we collect your name, email address, and password to set up and secure your account.
  • b. Event Information: We collect details about the events you browse, bookmark, book, or review, including location, date, and event type.
  • c. Payment Information: When confirming event bookings, we collect payment details (such as credit card information) to process transactions securely. Payment data is stored and handled by third-party payment processors.
  • d. Location Data: If you allow location services, we collect your device's location to recommend nearby events or enhance location-based features.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to deliver the best experience possible:

  • a. Event Recommendations: We use your preferences, event history, and location data to suggest relevant events tailored to your interests.
  • b. Booking Management: We help you track, manage, and edit your event bookings. You can cancel bookings within the terms specified by event organizers.
  • c. Secure Payments: We process payments through encrypted transactions to ensure the security of your personal and financial information.
  • d. Communication and Support: We may send you important updates, booking confirmations, reminders, and event suggestions. You may also receive promotional materials related to Kids App.

4. Sharing Your Information

We value your privacy and only share your information when necessary:

  • a. Event Organizers: When you book an event, relevant information such as your name and booking details may be shared with event organizers to confirm your attendance.
  • b. Third-Party Services: We may use third-party services (e.g., payment processors, customer support tools) to provide you with a seamless experience. These third parties have access to your information only as necessary and in line with their privacy policies.
  • c. Public Reviews: If you submit a review for an event, your username and review content will be visible to other users on Kids App.

5. Your Choices and Controls

Kids App gives you full control over your data:

  • a. Account Settings: You can update your profile, review preferences, and manage privacy settings within the app.
  • b. Notification Preferences: You can choose the types of notifications and promotional messages you want to receive in your notification settings.
  • c. Access and Deletion Requests: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you or request the deletion of your data by contacting our support team (details below).

6. Security Measures

We take data security seriously and have implemented industry-standard measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. Our app uses encryption and secure authentication methods to keep your personal data safe.

7. Children's Privacy

Kids App is designed for parents and guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will delete it immediately. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us to resolve the issue.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

Kids App may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or services. When we make updates, we will notify you within the app or via email. Please review this policy periodically for any changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
https://netrosystems.com/contact-us

10. Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your Kids App account, you can request deletion by sending an email to support@netrosystems.com. Alternatively, you may approach us directly through our website: netrosystems.com. Upon receiving your request, we will process the deletion in accordance with our data retention policies and applicable laws.
